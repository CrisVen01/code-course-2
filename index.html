Why to adopt a Pet?

"Dogs, cats and birds can be a great addition to the family. But why adopt a pet when you can buy one? The most plausible answer is that pets are neither objects nor products. They are living beings who deserve love and affection, and this is not measured financially. Here are some answers to the question " Why to adopt a pet?". .

Because you are saving a life: Each year millions of animals are born on the streets or are abandoned. Shelters and animal care procedures cannot contain demand. If more people adopt when buying, that number will drop considerably. An animal adopted in a shelter leaves a vacancy for another to be rescued.

Because you will have an excellent animal: Rescued animals in shelters often undergo examinations, vaccinations and castration. Many just were “left” after a divorce or a change. That is, we are already trained and used to living with a family.

Because it costs less: As stated above, many shelters already deliver castrated, vaccinated and dewormed animals. They are less costs for the beginning of life with the pet. And, of course, you are not spending on buying a puppy. Some races cost thousands of dollars. Because it is a way to combat trade and exploitation The horrors found in places that breed dogs and cats for sale are unspeakable. Yes, there are places like that.
